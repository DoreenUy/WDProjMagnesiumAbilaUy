The Magic Begins

The Beginning of a Legend

The Harry Potter franchise started as a book, written by a British writer, Joanne Rowling—better known as J. K. Rowling—during the 1900s. She wrote seven books about a boy wizard on a train heading toward a magical school called Hogwarts. The story was later adapted into eight films, as the final book was split into two parts. The first movie was released in the early 2000s.

Harry Potter and the Philosopher’s / Sorcerer’s Stone

In this movie, we are introduced to Harry Potter who is living with his abusive foster family. One day, he discovers that he has been invited to attend Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry and that he is famous in the wizarding world for being the boy who lived. He also forms a close relationship with Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger while trying to uncover his past. The trio learned that there was someone plotting to steal an important artifact hidden in the school. They worked together to prevent the Stone from falling into the wrong hands. Harry defeated Professor Quirrell, who was helping Voldemort, using the protection spell that his mother had cast before she died. In the end, Professor Dumbledore gives additional points to Gryffindor because of the bravery of the main trio which let them win the house cup. This movie marks the beginning of the journey of Harry Potter.

Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets

The movie starts as we see Harry missing his friends and school life in Hogwarts. Later on, he is warned by Dobby not to return to Hogwarts, but he refuses to listen which pushes the elf to get Harry in trouble so that he cannot return to school. He remains locked in his room until Ron breaks him out and takes him to their home. He remains locked in his room until Ron breaks him out and takes him to their home. During the new school year, students begin to be mysteriously petrified. Harry discovers he has the rare but dark ability of being able to speak Parseltongue and becomes determined to uncover the truth about the hidden Chamber within the school. Inside the chamber, Harry finds out that Tom Riddle is the younger version of Voldemort who explains how he was responsible for manipulating Ginny Weasley, Ron’s younger sister, into opening the chamber and releasing the basilisk. Harry defeated the Basilisk using the sword of Godrif Gryffindor. The trio is again rewarded for their bravery and the school is safe once more.

Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban

The movie starts as Harry once again escapes from his abusive household. Upon returning to Hogwarts, he learns that a dangerous prisoner named Sirius Black has escaped from Azkaban and is out to kill Harry to avenge Voldemort. On the train towards Hogwarts, Harry encounters Dementors, the guards of Azkaban. Then, he meets Professor Lupin, their new Defense Against the Dark Arts teacher, who protects him from the Dementors. During the school year, Hagrid introduces Buckbeak, a Hippogriff, to the class. However, Buckbeak was sentenced to be executed because Draco provoked the Hippogriff and got attacked by it. As the year goes on, the trio discovers that Sirius Black is actually innocent and the real traitor was Peter Pettigrew. Using the Time-Turner, Harry and Hermione go back in time to save Sirius and release Buckbeak. He also learned how to defeat Dementors using a Patronus which shows his huge advancement in his magic studies. Sirius escapes on Buckbeak, allowing him to remain free while Harry returns to Hogwarts knowing the truth about his godfather.

Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire

In this movie, Hogwarts hosts the Triwizard Tournament, where 3 challenges must be completed by one representative from each of the three schools. Students who are at least 17 years old and above are allowed to place their name in the Goblet of Fire and have the chance to be randomly chosen to represent their school. Cedric Diggory was chosen to represent Hogwarts, Viktor Krum was chosen to represent Drumstrang, and Fleur Delacour to represent Beauxbatons. Harry is then suddenly chosen by the goblet as the fourth representative for the tournament. Despite the suspicions of other students, Harry is able to survive all three challenges which include, dragons, underwater rescues, and a magical maze. During the maze challenge, Harry and Cedric are teleported to a graveyard where Lord Voldemort appears. Voldemort kills Cedric and attacks Harry, who was able to escape with Cedric’s body. When they return, it is also revealed that the Professor Moody who has been teaching Defence Against the Dark Art for this school year was actually a Dementor in disguise. The real Professor Moody is rescued and the truth comes out about how all events leading up to the confrontation between Harry and Voldemort were strategically planned to mark the return to power of the Dark Lord.

Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix

In this movie, we are introduced to the Order of The Phoenix founded by Professor Dumbledore. The Ministry of Magic refuses to believe that Harry is telling the truth about Voldemort’s return. In the new school year, the ministry assigns Dolores Umbridge as the new Defence Against the Dark Arts teacher. However, she does not teach practical magic in her class. This pushes Harry, Ron, and Hermione to form a group named Dumbledore’s Army to teach students practical magic that they can use to defend themselves. However, the students are eventually caught by Professor Umbridge and Slytherin students who helped her. This forced Professor Dumbledore to escape and held consequences for the students involved. Then, Harry had a vision of Sirius getting tortured by Voldemort and wanted to save him. He and his friends tricked Umbridge into getting caught by centaurs and made their way to the Ministry of Magic. A battle ensues between Dumbledore’s Army and the Death Eaters. During this battle, Sirius is killed by Bellatrix Lestrange which leaves Harry filled with grief. Another battle occurs between Dumbledore and Voldemort, and the ministry makes it in time to see that the Dark Lord really has returned.
