Hogwarts Traditions & Activities

  • Sorting Ceremony: First-years are sorted into their houses by the Sorting Hat.
  • Quidditch: Students play the magical sport with broomsticks and enchanted balls.
  • House Points: Earned or lost for behavior, academics, and achievements.
  • Feasts & Celebrations: Seasonal feasts, the Yule Ball, and special events like Halloween.
  • O.W.L.s and N.E.W.T.s: Standardized magical exams for students in their fifth and seventh years.
  • Magical Clubs: Duelling Club, Gobstones Club, and more for extracurricular practice.

Half-human, half-horse creatures known for wisdom and archery skills.
