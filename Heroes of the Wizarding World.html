Main Heroes

Harry Potter

Role: The central protagonist known as “The Boy Who Lived.” He survived the attack of Voldemort as a baby and is famous throughout the wizarding world for this.

Age per movie: 11–17

Achievements: Led the fight against Voldemort, won the Triwizard Tournament, defeated Death Eaters multiple times, became an Auror, and helped rebuild the wizarding world.

Hermione Granger

Role: Harry's brilliant and fiercely loyal friend, known for her intelligence, diligence, and dedication to justice. A key member of Dumbledore's Army and the trio.

Age per movie: 11–17

Achievements: Played a crucial role in finding and destroying Horcruxes, excelled academically, fought in numerous battles, and became a leading figure in the Ministry of Magic.

Ron Weasley

Role: Harry's loyal best friend, known for bravery, humor, and unwavering loyalty to his friends and family. Often acts as the emotional heart of the trio.

Age per movie: 11–17

Achievements: Assisted in destroying Horcruxes, fought in key battles, excelled at wizard chess, and later became an Auror and respected member of wizarding society.

Luna Lovegood

Role: Hogwarts student, eccentric and insightful, known for her unique perspective and unwavering loyalty to her friends. Member of Dumbledore's Army.

Age per movie: 14–16

Achievements: Fought in the Battle of Hogwarts, discovered key information, supported the trio, and became a famous Magizoologist in later life.

Cedric Diggory

Role: Hogwarts champion, brave Hufflepuff student, known for fairness, sportsmanship, and loyalty.

Age per movie: 17

Achievements: Triwizard co-champion, displayed exemplary courage and fairness, remembered as a symbol of honor and bravery in the wizarding world.

Villains

Tom Riddle

Role: Young Voldemort, a brilliant and manipulative student at Hogwarts. Ambitious, cunning, and skilled in dark magic, he began building the foundations of his future reign of terror.

Age per movie: 11–16

Achievements: Created Horcruxes while still a student, manipulated peers, uncovered ancient magical knowledge, and started laying the groundwork to become Voldemort.

Voldemort

Role: Dark Lord and main antagonist. Obsessed with immortality, power, and the domination of the wizarding world, known for cruelty and fearlessness in dark magic.

Age per movie: 71+

Achievements: Created multiple Horcruxes, led Death Eaters, nearly conquered the wizarding world, instilled fear across generations, and became the most notorious dark wizard in history.

Bellatrix Lestrange

Role: Loyal and fanatical Death Eater, devoted to Voldemort. Known for her cruelty, cunning, and obsession with pure-blood supremacy.

Age per movie: 45+

Achievements: Tortured prisoners, killed Sirius Black, fought in major battles, and spread terror throughout the wizarding world.

Peter Pettigrew

Role: Traitorous Death Eater, responsible for betraying the Potters. Cowardly and self-serving, but surprisingly skilled at concealment and stealth.

Age per movie: 30s

Achievements: Betrayed James & Lily Potter, served Voldemort for years, and caused significant harm through deception and espionage.

Draco Malfoy

Role: Slytherin student, rival of Harry Potter. Influenced by family and Slytherin ambition, he often struggled with morality and loyalty.

Age per movie: 11–17

Achievements: Participated in Death Eater schemes, served as a foil to Harry and friends, and ultimately survived while learning lessons about choices and consequences.

Professor Quirinus Quirrell

Role: Hogwarts teacher with a secret allegiance to Voldemort. Nervous and timid in appearance, but secretly ambitious and dangerous.

Age per movie: 30s–40s

Achievements: Attempted to steal the Philosopher’s Stone, hosted Voldemort on his head, and was ultimately defeated by Harry Potter, demonstrating courage and ingenuity on both sides.
